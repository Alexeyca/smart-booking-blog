We're launching the GoChatTravel blog. Here we'll share product updates, 
travel tips, and the story behind building a chat-first travel platform.

## The Problem

Booking travel still means: download an app, create an account, fill out forms, 
compare tabs, get redirected, repeat. Every trip starts with friction.

## What GoChatTravel Does

GoChatTravel lets you book hotels, flights, tours, and trains **directly in your 
messaging app** — WhatsApp, Telegram, Facebook Messenger, WeChat, or Instagram.

No new apps. No registrations. No redirects. The entire booking — from search to 
payment to confirmation — happens inside the chat.

## How It Works

1. Open your favorite messenger
2. Tell our AI assistant where you want to go (or just say "warm getaway with ocean views")
3. Get personalized options, pick one, pay — all in the same conversation
4. Instant confirmation, right in the chat thread

## What Makes It Different

- **AI Curation** — not a list of 500 hotels. Tailored picks based on what you actually want

- **Zero friction** — no app downloads, no logins, no form-filling
